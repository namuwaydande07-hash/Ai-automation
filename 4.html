Finance Sector

Saving 20,000 Man-Hours Per Year

Client: Multinational insurance company Challenge: Manual claims processing

The Challenge

Processing claims required manual verification of documents and data entry across 3 different systems.

The Solution

We implemented an RPA bot integrated with an AI OCR (Optical Character Recognition) system to extract data, cross-reference policy details, and approve claims.

The Results

  • 80% reduction in processing time.
  • 40% decrease in human error.
  • Employee satisfaction increased as staff moved to customer-facing roles.
